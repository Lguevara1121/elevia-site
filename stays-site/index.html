FAQ

What people ask us.

Elevia Stays is a venture of Elevia Enterprises developing premium furnished housing for people in transition — including insurance relocation housing, corporate housing, and furnished extended-stay rentals.

Elevia Stays serves displaced families navigating insurance claims, corporate travelers and relocating professionals on extended assignments, and individuals between permanent homes.

Elevia Stays is building its property network across Atlanta, Orlando, Tampa, Dallas, and Phoenix — with a long-term vision of expanding nationwide, and ultimately worldwide, in line with Elevia Enterprises’ mission to become a global force for opportunity.

Elevia Stays works with insurance adjusters and ALE (Additional Living Expense) providers to place displaced policyholders into fully furnished housing, typically for 30–90+ days, with a fast, seamless placement process.

Whether you’re an insurance professional, a corporate or HR team, a property owner, or navigating a transition yourself, reach out through our contact form and our team will follow up to discuss next steps.

Property owners can partner with Elevia Stays to generate consistent rental income through professionally managed insurance relocation, corporate housing, and extended-stay placements across our target markets.

Long-distance moves are costly and stressful — shipping furniture, buying everything new, or living out of boxes while you search for a permanent home. Elevia Stays lets you land in a fully furnished space right away, settle in immediately, and take your time finding the right long-term place, without the pressure or expense of moving everything at once.
